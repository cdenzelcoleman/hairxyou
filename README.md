## How to Play

1. **Create Your Character**: Click "Create Character" and choose your skin tone, hair texture, and hair color
2. **Style Your Hair**: Try the Hair Care routine, then pick a hairstyle in Hair Styling
3. **Get Dressed**: Choose your favorite outfit in Dress Up
4. **Add Polish**: Paint your nails and apply makeup
5. **Save**: Click "Save My Character" to download your creation!

## Design Philosophy

**Representation Matters**: HairXYou celebrates Black girls with authentic representation of hair textures, protective styles, and diverse skin tones.

**Joyful Expression**: The game focuses on fun, creative self-expression without restrictions or judgment.

**Accessible**: Simple graphics, web-based, works on any device with a modern browser.

**Educational**: Introduces hair care concepts and celebrates protective styling traditions.
